How to Play:

Start the game by clicking the large potato at the top of the page. Clicking gives you one potato. You use potatoes to buy the structures. These structures help you by giving you potatoes every second. Every time you buy a structure, the price goes up by 5%. You need to buy the highest cost of structures to get the next tier. Upgrades help you. Rewards are there to award you for your great doings! They will pop up once you reach it. Events occur somewhat rarely and they can be beneficial or bad.


Event Glossary

Potato Blight: Background turns purple with disease. Potato per seconds drop by 25%. 0.25% chance of happening per second.

Potato Bloom: Background turns light yellow with flowers. Potato per seconds increase by 10%. 0.5% chance of happening per second.

Robbery: Background turn dark blue with crime. Total potatoes drop by 10%. 0.25% chance of happening per second.

NUCLEAR EXPLOSION: Background turns red of nuclear power. Potato per seconds drop to 5%. 5e-5% chance of happening per second.

Mass Robbery: Background turns dark gray with lots of crime. Total potatoes drop to 0. 0.1% chance of happening per second.

Generous Donation: Background turns lime with money: Total potatoes increase by 20%. 0.3% chance of happening per second.

Very Generous Donation: Background turns light green with lots of money. Total potatoes increase by twice-fold. 0.1% chance of happening per second.

Potato Slaughter: Background turns red-orange with blood. Total potatoes drop by 80%. 0.15% chance of happening per second.
